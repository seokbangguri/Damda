Welcome to the website of the Modern History Museum of Art, Damda.

Our museum displays paintings related to modern and contemporary history of Korea.
It is an art museum as a cultural complex where we talk about history through paintings and communicate with neighbors through paintings.

In addition to exhibitions, the museum also runs cultural experience programs such as drawing, small concerts, humanities lectures, poetry recitals, and reading meetings.
It will be an art museum that creates a warm and happy world together by providing opportunities for social consideration and underprivileged families to enjoy culture together, and opportunities for artists to share the joy of creativity.
We are planning to carry out various projects such as the International Art Exchange Exhibition, the Inter-Korean Art Exchange Exhibition, and the May 18 National Painting Competition. I ask for your continued interest and encouragement.
