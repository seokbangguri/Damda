Purpose of establishment


1. Based on the modern and contemporary history of Korea, the "Museum of Art" aims to contribute to respect for life, mutual understanding, conflict resolution, peace on the Korean Peninsula, and world peace through various cultural and artistic projects.
2. By providing citizens and students with easy access to Korea's modern and contemporary history through paintings, they not only raise awareness of Korea's increasingly forgotten modern history but also take responsibility as the subjective owners of history.
3. Promote proper history education and patriotism, promote national culture and contribute to cultural development through wartime culture.
4. Emotional and personality education shall be provided to citizens and students and the base of culture and arts shall be expanded through appreciation of works, related photographs, materials, experience, etc.
5. Contribute to the creative activities of artists and artists.
